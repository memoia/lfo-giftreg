Notify Attendees

The idea here is to have a form for writing an email to all attendees of a given event, informing them to come to this site. Automatically-created users would receive an additional blurb containing their initial password (which for now is just "password" sans-quotes).

Emailing a notification wasn't explicitly part of the spec so it's omitted for now.

Constant passwords are bad, and storing generated passwords is also bad. Most likely would have to re-work this a bit to send an email notification to newly created accounts as they were created w/ initial password..
